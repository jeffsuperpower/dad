# Social Listening

## Purpose

On-demand scan of trending health topics, cultural moments, and social conversations across platforms. Feeds findings directly into content ideation — not a standalone report.

## What to Scan

### Platforms & Sources

| Source | What to Look For |
|--------|-----------------|
| **Google Trends** | Rising health search terms, breakout topics |
| **Twitter/X** | Trending health conversations, viral takes, discourse spikes |
| **TikTok** | Trending health content, new formats gaining traction |
| **Reddit** (r/health, r/biohackers, r/longevity, r/supplements) | Recurring questions, anxieties, debates |
| **Instagram** | Competitor content performing well, emerging visual trends |
| **News outlets** | Health news gaining mainstream attention |

Feed findings directly to the requesting agent:

```markdown

### Trending Topics (Top 5)
1. **[Topic]** — [Where it's trending] — [Why it matters]
2. ...

### Rising Conversations
- [Conversation or debate gaining traction]
- [Recurring question or anxiety]

### Authority Signals
- [What Huberman/Attia/etc are talking about this week]

### Competitor Watch
- [Notable content from Function/Healf/Everlab]

### Cultural Moments (Next 2 Weeks)
- [Upcoming health awareness days, events, seasonal moments]
```

## Key Principle

Surface what's emerging, not what's saturated. Early signals are more valuable than mainstream trends.
