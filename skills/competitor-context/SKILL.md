## Key Messaging Angles

### Angle 1: "Testing Is Just Step 1"

**Insight:** People think getting tested is the solution. It's actually just the beginning.

**Messaging:**
- "Testing is step 1. Knowing what to do about it? That's where most companies stop."
- "You don't need more data. You need a plan."
- "100+ biomarkers without a plan = expensive confusion"

**Hook examples:**
- "Your test results came back. Now what?"
- "Congrats, you got tested. Now what?"
- "Data without direction is just noise."

### Angle 2: "Detect vs. Fix"

**Insight:** Detection without action is meaningless.

**Messaging:**
- "Don't just detect heart disease risk—lower it."
- "Don't just find the problem. Fix it."
- "We don't stop at 'here's what's wrong.' We help you address it."

**Hook examples:**
- "They tell you what's wrong. We help you fix it."
- "Detection is easy. Action is everything."
- "What good is knowing if you can't do anything about it?"

### Angle 3: "The $199 vs $365 Value Gap"

**Insight:** Competitors charge more for less.

**Messaging:**
- "Why pay $365 for just data when $199 gets you data + a plan + support?"
- "$199 with protocols and clinical access vs $365 with... data."
- "More value. Less money. Actually helpful."

**Hook examples:**
- "$365 for testing. $199 for testing + a plan + clinical support. Easy math."
- "Some companies charge $365 to leave you confused. We charge $199 to help you improve."

### Angle 4: "The Google Spiral Problem"

**Insight:** People get test results and spiral into confusion or anxiety.

**Hook examples:**

### Angle 5: "The Full Stack"

**Insight:** Testing companies offer one piece. Superpower offers the full solution.

**Messaging:**
