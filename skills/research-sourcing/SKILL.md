# Research Sourcing

## Purpose

Find novel, consumer-relevant scientific research for use across social channels. Supports both ideation (finding topics) and production (sourcing evidence for content).

## Search Strategy

### Priority Sources
- **PubMed / NCBI** — Peer-reviewed medical research
- **Nature / Science** — High-impact journals
- **JAMA / NEJM / Lancet / BMJ** — Top medical journals
- **Cochrane Reviews** — Systematic reviews and meta-analyses
- **Cell / Cell Metabolism** — Cutting-edge biology
- **Preprint servers** (bioRxiv, medRxiv) — Flag as pre-peer-review

### Topics Relevant to Superpower

| Core Topics | Adjacent Topics |
|-------------|-----------------|
| Sleep & circadian biology | Cold/heat exposure |
| Diet & nutrition | Peptides & regulation |
| Exercise & performance | Wearables & healthtech |
| Hormone health | Microbiome research |
| Heart & metabolic health | Longevity interventions |
| Immune function | Environmental toxins |
| Gut health | Cellular health & epigenetics |
| Preventive diagnostics | Mental health & cognition |
| Biomarker science | Reproductive health |
| Supplements | Skin & aging |

### Quality Filters

**Include research that is:**
- Novel or surprising — advances understanding, not just confirms known facts
- Consumer-relevant — applies to everyday health decisions
- Actionable — the reader can do something with this information
- From reputable, peer-reviewed sources

**Exclude research that is:**
- About devices, treatments, or therapeutics not available to consumers
- Highly niche clinical trials with no consumer application
- Confirming well-established facts without new insight
- From predatory or low-credibility journals

### Recency
- **Prioritize last 3-6 months** for New Research content formats
- **Older research acceptable** for Health Education and foundational explainers
- **Always note publication date** in output

## Output Format

For each piece of research found:

```markdown
### [Paper Title — Simplified]
- **Source:** [Journal, Publication Date]
- **Finding:** [1-2 sentence plain-English summary]
- **Why it matters:** [Consumer relevance / actionability]
- **Superpower connection:** [Relevant biomarkers or panels, if any]
- **Link:** [URL]
```

Aim for 5-10 high-quality sources per research session.
